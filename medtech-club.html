Key Initiatives & Events

Physician-Engineer Dialogues

Organized panel discussions featuring surgeons, radiologists, and biomedical engineers to bridge clinical needs with engineering solutions. These sessions provide students with direct insight into real-world medical challenges.

Hands-on Workshops

Led practical workshops on medical device prototyping, including CAD design for implants, sensor integration for diagnostic devices, and regulatory pathways for medical technologies.

Industry Networking

Facilitated connections with medical device companies including guest speakers from Intuitive Surgical, Medtronic, and Boston Scientific, providing members with career development opportunities.

Leadership & Impact

Club Foundation

Founded the first MedTech-focused student organization at NYU Tandon, developing the constitution, securing faculty sponsorship, and building the initial membership base from scratch.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

Established partnerships with NYU Grossman School of Medicine and NYU College of Dentistry, creating unique opportunities for engineering students to engage with medical students and clinicians.

Project Incubation

Mentored student teams developing medical devices, including a low-cost prosthetic hand, smart pill dispenser, and portable diagnostic device for global health applications.

Interested in Medical Device Innovation?

The NYU Tandon MedTech Club welcomes students from all backgrounds who are passionate about improving healthcare through technology.
