My journey in medical device engineering

My interest in medical devices was first an interest in becoming a doctor. During my last year of high school, I realized that I could enter the medical field without having to be a doctor. Being an engineer that makes the tools in a clinical environment was a very interesting pathway that I wanted to explore.
